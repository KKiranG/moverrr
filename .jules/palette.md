## 2026-04-12 - Explicit Focus States on Custom Selectable Buttons
**Learning:** This app uses custom `div` and `<button>` elements to create rich, multi-select pill and card interfaces (e.g., Detour Radius Presets, Accept Options). While they look clickable, they often miss out on standard keyboard navigation feedback because they don't use the standard generic `<Button>` component or don't explicitly apply `focus-visible` styles on their own. This causes accessibility regressions for users tabbing through the UI.
**Action:** When implementing custom clickable selection cards or preset buttons in this codebase, explicitly include standard focus styling (`focus-visible:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-accent/30 transition-colors`) to maintain keyboard navigation consistency with primary buttons.

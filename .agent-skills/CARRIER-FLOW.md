# Carrier Flow

## Onboarding

1. Sign up with email and password.
2. Enter business and contact details.
3. Register at least one vehicle.
4. Upload licence and insurance.
5. Wait for manual verification.

## Post Capacity

Three-screen wizard:

1. Route
   - origin suburb
   - destination suburb
   - detour radius
2. When and space
   - trip date
   - time window
   - space size
   - max weight
3. Price and rules
   - carrier-set price
   - accepted item types
   - stairs
   - helper
   - special notes

## Dashboard Jobs To Be Done

- see active listings
- review bookings on each trip
- upload pickup and delivery proof
- track upcoming payouts

## UX principles

- mobile-first
- minimal admin overhead
- clear price guidance
- no unnecessary chat

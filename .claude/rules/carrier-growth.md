# Carrier Growth Rules

Carrier growth is moverrr's primary flywheel.

## Priorities

- post real supply quickly
- repeat proven routes with minimal typing
- surface weak-listing fixes clearly
- keep ops expectations legible after publish
- make today-level operational blockers obvious without drifting into dispatch

## Product Shape

- templates help repeat supply
- do not drift into dispatch tooling
- guidance should stay explainable, not black-box

## Verification

- carrier can post quickly on mobile
- weak listings are warned or blocked
- repeat-post and template flows reduce friction

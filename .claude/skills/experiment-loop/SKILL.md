# Experiment Loop

Borrow the `autoresearch` discipline: small loop, explicit baseline, one meaningful variable, evidence-based keep or discard.

## Output Shape

For each proposed experiment, define:

1. **Baseline**
   What is true right now?
2. **Hypothesis**
   What do we think will improve?
3. **Single major change**
   What is the one variable we are changing?
4. **Time box**
   What is the smallest useful effort window?
5. **Success signal**
   Which number or behavior tells us it worked?
6. **Keep / discard rule**
   What result means keep, and what result means revert or defer?

## Good MoveMate Experiment Targets

- carrier time-to-post
- saved-search capture rate
- search-to-book conversion
- proof completion rate
- dispute resolution time
- admin queue time
- carrier re-post frequency

## Rules

- do not mix three unknowns into one experiment
- prefer reversible changes
- record the learning even when the experiment fails
- favor experiments that strengthen supply, trust, or clarity first
